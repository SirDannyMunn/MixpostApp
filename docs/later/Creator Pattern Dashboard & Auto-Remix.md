# 1. Creator Pattern Dashboard

**Purpose:** Turn raw social chaos into a readable, repeatable playbook.

This is **not analytics**. It’s a **pattern intelligence cockpit**.

## What it answers (the questions creators actually ask)

* What hooks are winning *right now* in my niche?
* Which angles are saturated vs underused?
* What offers convert without killing reach?
* Which creators are worth copying—and why?
* What format should I post *today* if my goal is X?

---

### B. Angle Saturation Map

**What you show**
Angles plotted on a 2D grid:

* X-axis: repetition rate
* Y-axis: engagement efficiency

**Quadrants**

* **Gold**: high engagement, low saturation
* **Crowded**: high engagement, high saturation
* **Dead**: low engagement, high saturation
* **Emerging**: low engagement, low saturation

**Example angles (SEO niche)**

* “AI replaces SEO teams” → Crowded
* “n8n SEO agents” → Emerging
* “Free courses” → Dead

## What Auto-Remix Is (and Isn’t)

❌ Not: “Rewrite this post”
❌ Not: Madlibs content

✅ Is: **Controlled mutation of proven structures**

---

## Remix Inputs (Already in Your System)

* Hook archetype
* Angle cluster
* Format
* Emotional driver
* Audience sophistication
* Offer mechanics

---

## Remix Rule Examples

### Rule 1: Hook Mutation

**Base hook**

> “I replaced my SEO team with AI.”

**Variants**

* Compression: “40 hours of SEO work → 4 hours with AI.”
* Fear: “Manual SEO is why most sites stagnate.”
* Status: “This is how top agencies run SEO now.”

Same idea. Different entry point.

---

### Rule 2: Format Transcoding

One idea → many outputs.

**Source**
Framework thread

**Auto outputs**

* X thread (5 tweets)
* LinkedIn carousel outline
* Short-form video script
* Newsletter intro
* CTA-less authority post

This is **repurposing as code**, not manual work.

---

### Rule 3: Angle × Emotion Matrix

Take one angle, run it through emotions:

| Angle           | Emotion | Result                              |
| --------------- | ------- | ----------------------------------- |
| AI replaces SEO | Fear    | “Your SEO job is at risk.”          |
| AI replaces SEO | Relief  | “Finally, SEO doesn’t suck.”        |
| AI replaces SEO | Status  | “This is how top operators do SEO.” |

Each version hits a different segment.

---

### Rule 4: Offer Compatibility Guardrails

Prevent dumb outputs.

Example rule:

> “Do not attach aggressive CTAs to authority-first posts.”

Or:

> “Only allow DM-gated CTAs when hook intensity ≥ 0.7.”

This avoids killing reach with premature selling.

---

### Rule 5: Saturation-Aware Remixing

If angle fatigue is high:

* Shift format
* Shift emotion
* Soften claim
* Remove CTA

This lets you **stay in the conversation without being redundant**.
